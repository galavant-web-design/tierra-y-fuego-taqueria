Find the food truck!

We partner with local businesses and parks to share our taco love with different areas of Boulder. This way we get to mix things up, pairing our tacos with different local vendors, locations and public events!

View our public taco truck calendar to find out when and where we'll be slinging tacos next! You may find yourself at a park eating a taco after a hike or at a public event promoting one of your favorite organizations alongside Tierra y Fuego Taqueria.

Availability

If you didn't find a location you were hoping for, get in touch with our event coordinator to suggest a location for our taco truck.

Still need a taco fix? --visit our restaurant in NoBo or order takeout during regular business hours. Also, consider Tierra y Fuego catering for your next event!

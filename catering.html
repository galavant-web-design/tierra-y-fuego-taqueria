Taco Bar Catering

Bring the flavors of Tierra y Fuego Taqueria to your party! Whether you’d like to pick up, have us deliver, or even bring our fully staffed food trailer to your event, we can customize our catering to meet your needs.

Catering Menu

Our most basic catering starts at $12 per person and includes three tacos, beans, rice, choice of two mains, plus two salsas. Fresh chopped cilantro, onions and limes are also included. Private Events

The Diaz Farm has been home to Tierra y Fuego Taqueria’s food truck since 2018. Originally an outdoor public venue, the Diaz Farm now hosts private events seasonally with its Tierra y Fuego Taqueria food truck serving up traditional Mexican F&B for your special event.

We host a wide variety of events--from work events to milestone birthdays, graduation parties, or rehearsal dinners and can cater to your event’s style.
